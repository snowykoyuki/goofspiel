Play: Start the game.

Singleplayer:
You will play Goofspiel against a computer player. 

Multiplayer:
Play against a friend (or yourself).

Settings:
Enable or disable alternate gamemode.

The cards you have will be listed on the screen. Type the corresponding number for the card you wish to select, and press enter to send.
To confirm your card choice, enter y.
If you wish to change, enter n.

End your turn by pressing enter.

In multiplayer, there will be a screen before you begin your turn. This ensures that your opponent cannot see what cards you still have in posession.
